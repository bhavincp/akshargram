Animals, insects, plants and people are all fundamental parts of the cycle of natural farming. We see caring for the cows at our farm, gau seva, as a form of worship. Cows not only give us milk but also their dung and urine which are food for hungry land. Satisfied land gives us healthy food, which makes us stronger and more able to care for the land, the cows, and our community.
